Marvel Cinematic Universe Set in the dystopian world of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), this committee will explore the themes of AI, accountability, and abuse of power. The focus will be on Protocol for the use of AI by the Avengers after the events of Age of Ultron. Additionally, this committee will debate The Future of the Avengers, questioning whether it is an abuse of power and if the Avengers should be reformed or abolished in the face of the disaster left in their wake.

Topic 1: Protocol for the use of AI by the Avengers.
Topic 2: The Future of the Avengers.

Cold War

In this Cold War Model UN committee, delegates will address the escalating arms race and the political divide created by the Iron Curtain. The focus will be on the Nuclear Arms Race as it brings global security to the brink, while the Iron Curtain shapes diplomatic relations between East and West. Delegates will navigate tense alliances, proxy wars, and the threat of nuclear conflict. The committee will challenge participants to balance national interests with the need for diplomacy and peace. It’s a high-stakes environment where every decision could shift the course of history.

Topic 1: The Nuclear Arms Race
Topic 2: The Iron Curtain

As United States president Donald Trump takes office and threatens to place worldwide tariffs on all his allies, delegates must come together to discuss issues such as economic stability, the effects on the global job market, and potential trade conflicts. Additionally, this committee will debate about threats of annexation from the United States, questioning whether it’s too late for negotiations or if the only possible outcome is war.

Topic 1: The United States has threatened to impose 25% tariffs onto several countries
Topic 2: The United States has announced their plans to annex Greenland
